## Context
The platform currently uses "Lead" and "Leads" inconsistently alongside "Opportunity" and "Opportunities" in various places. The decision has been made to standardize all user-facing terminology to "Deal" and "Deals" across the entire platform. This applies everywhere a user can see text — no exceptions.

## Important Distinctions
- **Stage names are NOT changed** — "New Inquiry", "Consult Booked", "Quote Sent" etc. are stage names that describe where a deal is in the funnel. Do not rename these.
- **"Lead Source" field label** — rename to "Source" on all forms and panels for brevity. The Settings page section can remain "Lead Sources" as that is the name of the managed list.
- **Pipeline name** — "Surgical Pipeline", "Non-Surgical Pipeline" etc. are pipeline names, not record types. Do not change these.
